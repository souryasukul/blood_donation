FDIC Blood Donation Rules

  • You must be in good general health and feeling well.
  • You must be at least 16 years old (with parental consent in some states).
  • You must weigh at least 110 pounds.
  • You must not have donated blood in the last 56 days.
  • Bring a valid photo ID and stay hydrated before donating.
